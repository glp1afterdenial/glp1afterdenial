## The Math

### The Formula

**Step 1: Calculate concentration**
```
peptide (mg) √∑ water (mL) = mg per mL
```

**Step 2: Calculate units to draw**
```
desired dose (mg) √∑ concentration (mg/mL) √ó 100 = units
```

### Example

- **5mg vial** + **2mL water** = **2.5 mg/mL**
- Want **0.5mg** dose?
- 0.5 √∑ 2.5 √ó 100 = **20 units**

## Step-by-Step Reconstitution

<div style="background: #f0f9ff; border-radius: 12px; padding: 24px; margin: 24px 0;">

### Step 1: Prep
- Wash hands thoroughly
- Wipe both vial tops with alcohol swabs
- Let dry 10-15 seconds

### Step 2: Draw Water
- Pull back syringe plunger to desired amount
- Insert needle into BAC water vial
- Push air in, draw water out
- Tap out air bubbles

### Step 3: Add Water to Peptide

**‚ö†Ô∏è IMPORTANT:** Aim needle at the **glass wall**, not directly at the powder. Let water run down the side slowly.

### Step 4: Mix Gently

**DO NOT SHAKE.** Shaking damages the peptide.

- Swirl gently in circles, OR
- Just let it sit 5-15 minutes ‚Äî it dissolves on its own

Solution should be **clear**. If cloudy or has particles, don't use.

### Step 5: Label & Store
- Write date and concentration on vial
- **Refrigerate** (not freezer)
- Good for 4-6 weeks

### How to Inject

1. Clean site with alcohol swab, let dry
2. Pinch skin to create a fold
3. Insert needle at 45-90¬∞ angle
4. Release pinch
5. Push plunger slowly
6. Wait 5-10 seconds before removing
7. Don't rub the site

**Rotate sites** ‚Äî don't use the same spot twice in a row.
