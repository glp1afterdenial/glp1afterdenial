## Talk to a Doctor

Before starting, stopping, or changing any medication — including GLP-1 agonists — **consult with a qualified healthcare provider**.

This is especially important if you have:
- Diabetes or blood sugar issues
- Thyroid problems or family history of thyroid cancer
- Kidney or liver disease
- History of pancreatitis
- Any other medical conditions
