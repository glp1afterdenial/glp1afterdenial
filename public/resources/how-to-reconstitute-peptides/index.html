Step-by-Step Reconstitution #

Step 1: Prep #

  • Wash hands thoroughly
  • Wipe both vial tops with alcohol swabs
  • Let dry 10-15 seconds

Step 2: Draw Water #

  • Pull back syringe plunger to desired amount
  • Insert needle into BAC water vial
  • Push air in, draw water out
  • Tap out air bubbles

Step 3: Add Water to Peptide #

‚ö†Ô∏è IMPORTANT: Aim needle at the glass wall, not directly at the powder. Let water run down the side slowly.

Step 4: Mix Gently #

DO NOT SHAKE. Shaking damages the peptide.

  • Swirl gently in circles, OR
  • Just let it sit 5-15 minutes ‚Äî it dissolves on its own

Solution should be clear. If cloudy or has particles, don’t use.

Step 5: Label & Store #

  • Write date and concentration on vial
  • Refrigerate (not freezer)
  • Good for 4-6 weeks

Side Effects #

Common (especially when starting):

  • Nausea (worst first 1-2 days after injection)
  • Decreased appetite
  • Constipation or diarrhea
  • Fatigue

Managing them:

  • Start low, increase slowly
  • Stay hydrated
  • Eat protein even when not hungry
  • Some people inject at night to sleep through nausea
