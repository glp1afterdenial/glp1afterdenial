Frequently Asked Questions #

Is it legal to buy research peptides?

Research peptides are legal to purchase in the US for research purposes. They’re not FDA-approved for human use. The legal gray area is in how you use them.

How do I know the peptides are real?

Look for third-party COA (Certificate of Analysis) showing purity. Reputable vendors like Royal Peptides provide these for every batch.

Can I just inject the powder?

No. Peptides come as lyophilized (freeze-dried) powder. You must reconstitute them with bacteriostatic water first.

How should I store them?

  • Unreconstituted (powder): Room temperature is fine, refrigerator is better
  • Reconstituted (mixed): Must be refrigerated, use within 4-6 weeks
