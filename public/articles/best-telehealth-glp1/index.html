Brand vs Compounded: Does It Matter?

üíä Brand (Wegovy, Zepbound)

  • Made by pharma companies
  • FDA-approved
  • Consistent batch to batch
  • Pre-filled pens or vials
  • More expensive

üè• Compounded

  • Made by compounding pharmacies
  • Same active ingredient
  • Regulated by state boards
  • Usually in vials
  • Cheaper

Does it work the same? For most people, yes. But absorption may vary slightly. If switching from brand to compounded, pay attention to how your body responds.
