What I Wish I'd Known

üö® Protein and Hair Loss

Many people lose hair on GLP-1s. It's usually preventable.

When you eat way less (which these meds cause), you might not get enough protein, water, or vitamins. Hair loss is often the first sign you're undereating protein. Aim for 100+ grams/day.
